# SevaSetu Requirements

## Objective
Provide multilingual AI assistance for discovering and applying to government welfare schemes.

## Target Users
Citizens, students, elderly people, small business owners.

## Core Features
- Voice and text interaction
- Scheme eligibility recommendation
- Step-by-step application guidance
- Document validation
- Application status tracking
- Notifications via SMS or app

## Non Functional Requirements
- Secure
- Scalable
- Low bandwidth friendly
- Easy to use
